# Prompts para Mapas — "Herederos de las Ruinas"

Set de 7 prompts (1 mapa general + 6 mapas regionales) pensados para generar una serie de mapas consistentes entre sí. Usa la **Guía de estilo compartida** como base y pégala (o resúmela) junto con cada prompt regional para mantener coherencia visual.

---

## Guía de estilo compartida (incluir/repetir en cada prompt)

"Fantasy atlas map, hand-drawn cartography on aged parchment, ink linework with watercolor wash, ornate compass rose, weathered edges, sepia base tones with regional color accents, fine decorative border, top-down orthographic projection, no modern elements. Consistent icon language across the whole atlas: crown = imperial capital, tower/spire = colegio (academy), small heraldic banner = casa menor, stylized giant tree/landmark = Anclado (glowing for Ascendido, dark/cracked for Corrompido), spiral glyph = zona de Eco errático, dotted line = ruta de portal o migración estacional, sea-serpent silhouette = fauna colosal."

---

## Mapa 0 — Mapa General / Planetario

"Massive planetary-scale fantasy map, [Guía de estilo compartida]. The ENTIRE known civilized world — all six regions (El Cinturón Rojo, La Meseta Gris, Las Espinas, Los Mares Atados, El Cauce Errante, Las Tierras Quebradas) — appears as a single small landmass cluster occupying no more than 10-15% of the map, pushed toward one corner/edge. The remaining 85%+ is: vast uncharted ocean with faint sea-monster silhouettes half-visible beneath the waves, unexplored continents rendered only in outline with 'tierras sin cartografiar' labels, mountain ranges explicitly drawn as the backs of sleeping colossal beasts, and swirling spiral glyphs marking 'zonas de Eco errático'. 

Within the small known-world cluster, show the six regions in their rough relative arrangement: Las Espinas (jagged white mountains) to the east with Khaganato de Ossvell on cold steppes further north; La Meseta Gris (gray plains) central-west, bordering El Cinturón Rojo (red volcanic terrain) to its south/west; between Meseta Gris and Cinturón Rojo, mark a frontier zone for Imperio de Doranth with three small independent-college tower icons (Sin-Dios, Umbral Común, Séptima Era) and four unaffiliated house banners (Vey, Orrin, Stillwater, Embry); Los Mares Atados (foggy archipelago) to the north coast; El Cauce Errante (shifting desert/coastal terrain with dotted seasonal trails) to the south; Las Tierras Quebradas (cratered ruin-fields) to the west.

Add a corner cartouche titled 'Herederos de las Ruinas — Mapa del Mundo Conocido' with a small inset/locator box reinforcing how tiny the known world is relative to the full planet, plus the shared icon-legend key."

## Mapa 1 — El Cinturón Rojo (Sangre / Casa del Cántico Eterno)

"[Guía de estilo compartida]. Detailed regional map of El Cinturón Rojo: volcanic highlands, lava fields, red/orange rocky terrain, cracked obsidian plains. 

- Mark **Imperio de Vethranor** with a large crown icon over its capital city, rigid concentric district rings (visual cue for its strict blood-purity hierarchy).
- Three colegio tower icons, clearly labeled: **Colegio del Cántico Vivo** (training grounds with young Linaje figures), **Colegio de la Sangre Compartida** (large amphitheater for mass synchronization rituals), **Colegio de los Linajes Quebrados** (isolated fortress for Sin-Nombre).
- Anclados as glowing/dark landmark icons: **El Corazón de la Tierra** (incandescent root beneath a dormant volcano, mining settlements built into its root-system), **El Roble del Primer Juramento** (massive ancient oak at the border with La Meseta Gris, ritual grove around it), **La Zarza de Hierro** (dark corrupted thornbush fused with metal veins, deep mining zone, Corrompido glow).
- Corner cartouche with a sigil legend for the **16 Casas Menores afines**: Vael, Korrath, Dessenor, Hael-Mor, Mireth, Sovain, Tarnec, Issendral, Velkar, Ophrasil, Ravenhar, Quennith, Brennoc, Ysendra, Dorvain, Aelmor — each a small heraldic banner placed near plausible territories (mining zones, border garrisons, etc.).
- Color accent: deep reds, oranges, black volcanic rock, glowing ember highlights for eye-glow/blood motifs."

## Mapa 2 — La Meseta Gris (Estudio / Biblioteca Infinita) + Frontera de Doranth

"[Guía de estilo compartida]. Detailed regional map of La Meseta Gris: cold gray plains, frozen lakes, scattered standing-stone ritual circles, sparse tundra vegetation.

- Mark **Dominio de Cassyr-Ulen** capital with a crown icon atop a fortified academic city (visual cue: layered libraries/scriptoriums instead of palaces).
- Three colegio tower icons: **Colegio de Cassyr** (the largest structure on the map — an immense spiraling library-tower, central landmark), **Colegio de los Cartógrafos del Eco** (observatory-like tower surrounded by survey markers), **Colegio de la Glosa Muda** (isolated dark tower, restricted/quarantined area marked with warning glyphs).
- Anclados: **El Árbol del Mundo (Yggrasil Vethra)** — colossal tree whose canopy is visible from across the region, glowing Ascendido aura, scholar camps beneath its roots; **El Loto de Cassyr** — glowing lotus flowers on a frozen lake, ritual gardens around it.
- Along the southern/western edge, mark the **frontier with Imperio de Doranth**: a pragmatic walled border-city without strong Eco-identity iconography, plus nearby tower icons for the 3 independent colegios (Sin-Dios, Umbral Común, Séptima Era) and small banners for Casas independientes Vey, Orrin, Stillwater, Embry.
- Corner cartouche sigil legend for the **16 Casas Menores afines**: Velmoor, Threnn, Auberon, Pelliter, Greymont, Nyssa, Wrenfield, Corvale, Ashgrove, Tindell, Brackwell, Selwyn, Ormund, Fenwick, Calloway, Esterling.
- Color accent: cool grays, pale blues, muted parchment tones, silver ink details for inscriptions/runes."

## Mapa 3 — Las Espinas (Energía Interna / Vaso Sin Fondo) + Khaganato de Ossvell

"[Guía de estilo compartida]. Detailed regional map of Las Espinas: extreme jagged mountain range, snow-capped peaks, narrow valleys, monastery terraces carved into cliffs.

- Mark **Reino de las Siete Mareas** capital in a lower valley (warm, sheltered, accessible mainly in winter) with crown icon and monastery-fortress architecture.
- To the north, across colder steppes, mark **Khaganato de Ossvell** with yurts/clan-camp icons and a horse-warrior motif, visually rival/parallel to the Reino.
- Three colegio icons as mountain monasteries: **Colegio de las Siete Mareas** (classic central monastery), **Colegio de los Rompe-Mareas** (austere shock-troop barracks, higher altitude), **Colegio del Vaso Roto** (infirmary-like complex, lower altitude, healing motifs).
- Anclados: **El Pilar Blanco** (glowing white ice-tree on one face of the mountains, temperate valley around it), **La Espina Negra** (dark crystalline corrupted tree on the opposite face, jagged crystal growths), **La Espiral de Cuarzo** (colossal spiral lichen on the highest peaks, faint visible 'tone' rings), **El Cedro de las Siete Mareas** (ancient cedar with seven distinct ring-layers visible, in the same low valley as the Reino's capital).
- Corner cartouche sigil legend for the **16 Casas Menores afines**: Doruun, Yashiro, Korrigan, Ilvani, Bashtu, Renkai, Vorr, Tessandra, Olwen, Drathmore, Sennin, Whitlock, Quorrin, Belmara, Druvash, Yyrith.
- Color accent: whites, icy blues, dark grays/blacks for corrupted crystal zones, sharp high-contrast peaks."

## Mapa 4 — Los Mares Atados (Pacto / El Coro Silente) + Talasocracia de Ilvenmar y Korrabel

"[Guía de estilo compartida]. Detailed regional map of Los Mares Atados: archipelago of islands wrapped in perpetual fog, scattered port-cities, swamp interiors.

- Mark **Confederación de Maren** as a loose cluster of semi-independent port-city icons (no single capital crown — multiple smaller crowns/seals).
- Mark **Talasocracia de Ilvenmar** in open sea between Mares Atados and El Cauce Errante — a naval-empire icon (fleet formations, navigation-route dotted lines guided by coastal Anclados).
- Mark **Imperio Sumergido de Korrabel** as a partially-submerged city icon along the deep southern coast, near El Manglar Eterno — semi-amphibious architecture, visible both above and below the waterline.
- Three colegio icons: **Colegio del Susurro** (coastal tower wrapped in fog, whisper/spirit motifs), **Colegio del Grillete** (fortified island, chain/shackle iconography), **Colegio de las Voces Atadas** (archive-island, ledger/scroll motifs).
- Anclados: **La Madre de Niebla** (colossal coral/algae mass covering an entire archipelago in dense fog), **El Sauce de las Voces** (corrupted willow in interior swamps, faint 'whisper' lines drawn emanating from it), **El Faro Viviente** (glowing bioluminescent tree marking safe channels through the fog, facing La Meseta Gris coast), **El Manglar Eterno** (lush stable mangrove ecosystem on the southern coast, near Korrabel).
- In the surrounding ocean, depict kraken-like colossal silhouettes lurking beneath the fog banks — sea-monster motif for this region.
- Corner cartouche sigil legend for the **16 Casas Menores afines**: Vendrel, Ohmer, Fennick, Lysara, Brask, Drennor, Ithyl, Vorne, Sallowmere, Quill, Brindal, Yorrick, Pellan, Sevrin, Doone, Marrow.
- Color accent: teal, fog-gray, deep blue-green, pale bioluminescent highlights."

## Mapa 5 — El Cauce Errante (Elementos / Los Hijos del Cauce)

"[Guía de estilo compartida]. Detailed regional map of El Cauce Errante: shifting terrain that changes between coastal wetlands (winter) and interior deserts (summer) — render BOTH states subtly overlaid, connected by dotted seasonal migration trails.

- Mark **Hegemonía del Cauce** with a 'mobile capital' icon shown along its migration route (a caravan-city, not a fixed crown).
- Three colegio icons: **Colegio del Cauce Errante** (itinerant tent/wagon-city following the routes), **Colegio de los Pastores** (traditional nomad camps embedded with seminomadic communities), **Colegio de Cauces Robados** (artifact-assisted outpost, viewed with suspicion — slightly isolated).
- Anclados: **El Cauce Verde** (a moving prairie shown mid-migration, with visible 'trail' it leaves behind that overlaps the seasonal Eco routes), **El Devorador de Arena** (colossal semi-buried carnivorous plant in the desert zone, with a false-oasis around it and small offering-shrines), **El Bosque que Camina** (a forest visibly mid-stride at the border with Las Tierras Quebradas, trees angled as if walking away from something).
- In coastal waters, depict a colossal whale-titan migrating along the coast, leaving a visible shimmering 'wake' that represents the seasonal Eco shift.
- Corner cartouche sigil legend for the **16 Casas Menores afines**: Mireval, Sennar, Quillon, Brannigan, Estrel, Vannar, Holt, Maren, Corwin, Ashby, Tovrin, Linwood, Fenmar, Brael, Oswick, Dunmore.
- Color accent: ochre, sandy gold, seasonal green-to-brown gradient, teal coastal strip."

## Mapa 6 — Las Tierras Quebradas (Artefactos / La Mano de Cenizo)

"[Guía de estilo compartida]. Detailed regional map of Las Tierras Quebradas: cratered wastelands, scattered ruins, broken/uneven terrain, pockets of erratic Eco marked with spiral glyphs.

- Mark **Sindicato de Cenizo** as a network of mercantile city-state icons connected by trade-route dotted lines (not a single capital — emphasize the trading-network feel).
- Three colegio icons: **Colegio de la Hermandad del Fragmento** (archaeological dig-camp city, excavation pits), **Colegio de los Custodios de Cenizo** (sealed vault-fortress, heavily warded), **Colegio del Fragmento Mudo** (isolated research outpost near a 'mute ruins' field, working alongside Glosa Muda).

Optionally show the edge of **El Bosque que Camina** entering from the El Cauce Errante border.
- Corner cartouche sigil legend for the **16 Casas Menores afines**: Fenrow, Drevasque, Albrecht, Sorrowmere, Vexley, Brunhol, Ondrik, Petrelli, Sashka, Greaves, Ulfrich, Bellweather, Korrin, Maddox, Severin, Thorne.

## Notas de uso

- Genera primero el **Mapa 0** para fijar la composición/paleta general; luego los 6 regionales referenciando ese resultado como guía de estilo (muchos generadores permiten "image reference" o "style reference").
- Si el generador no soporta tanto texto/detalle en un solo render, prioriza: terreno + Anclados + capital imperial + colegios en el render principal, y deja las 16 casas menores como leyenda de cartucho (sigilos pequeños, no necesitan estar "in situ" con precisión geográfica).
- Pendiente para refinar más adelante: posiciones relativas exactas entre las 6 regiones (actualmente solo aproximadas según pistas del lore: Doranth entre Cinturón Rojo y Meseta Gris, Ossvell al norte de Las Espinas, Ilvenmar y Korrabel cerca de Mares Atados/Cauce Errante).
